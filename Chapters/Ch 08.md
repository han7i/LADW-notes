### 3. Adjoint (dual) transformations and transpose. Fundamental subspace revisited (once more)

> 3.3. Let $\mathbf{v}_1, \mathbf{v}_2, \ldots, \mathbf{v}_n$ be a basis in $X$ and let $\mathbf{v}_1^{\prime}, \mathbf{v}_2^{\prime}, \ldots, \mathbf{v}_n^{\prime}$ be its dual basis. Let $E:=\operatorname{span}\left\{\mathbf{v}_1, \mathbf{v}_2, \ldots, \mathbf{v}_r\right\}, r<n$. Prove that $E^{0}=\operatorname{span}\left\{\mathbf{v}_{r+1}^{\prime}, \ldots, \mathbf{v}_n^{\prime}\right\}$.

考虑 $F \subseteq E^{\ast},$ 令 \[
 \forall \mathbf{x} \in E \, \forall \mathbf{y} \in F, \mathbf{y}(\mathbf{x}) = 0,
\] 即 \[
 \sum^n_{i=1} b_i\mathbf{v}_i^\prime\left(\sum^r_{j=1}a_j\mathbf{v}_j\right)  = \sum^r_{i=1} b_i a_i = 0
\] 由 $a_i$ 的任意性知 $b_i = 0, \forall i \in [1,r]_\mathbb{Z},$ 故 $E^0 \subseteq \operatorname{span}\{\mathbf{v}_{r+1}, \ldots , \mathbf{v}_n\};$
反之类似地有 $\operatorname{span}\{\mathbf{v}_{r+1}, \ldots , \mathbf{v}_n\} \subseteq E^0.$ 

### 4. What is the difference between a space and its dual?

> 4.1. Let $D$ be a differential operator \[
 D = \sum^n_{k=1} v_k \frac{\partial}{\partial x_k}.
\] Show, using the chain rule, that if we change a basis and write $D$ in new coordinates, its coefficients $v_k$ change according to the change of coordinates rule for vectors.

考虑一组新的坐标 $\{\widetilde{x}_1, \ldots ,\widetilde{x}_n\},$ 由链式法则，\[
 D = \sum^n_{k=1} v_k \sum^n_{j=1} \frac{\partial \widetilde{x}_j}{\partial x_j} \frac{\partial}{\partial \widetilde{x}_k} = \sum^n_{k=1} \widetilde{v}_k \frac{\partial}{\partial \widetilde{x}_k}, \\
 \widetilde{v}_k = \sum^n_{j=1} \frac{\partial \widetilde{x}_j}{\partial x_j} v_k, k \in [1,n]_{\mathbb{Z}},
\] 而将 $D$ 视为切空间中的向量，基底 $\{ \frac{\partial}{\partial x} \}$ 到 $\{ \frac{\partial}{\partial \widetilde{x}} \}$ 的换基矩阵即雅可比矩阵，与上式一致。

### 5. Multilinear functions. Tensors

> 5.2 Show that the set $\{ \mathbf{v}_1 \otimes \mathbf{v}_2 \otimes \cdots \otimes \mathbf{v}_p : \mathbf{v}_k \in V_k \}$ of tensor product of vectors is strictly less than $V_1 \otimes V_2 \otimes \cdots \otimes V_p.$

\[\begin{aligned}
 \mathbf{v}_1 \otimes \cdots \otimes \mathbf{v}_p &= \left(\sum_{i_1} a_{1,i_1}\mathbf{b}^{\,1}_{i_1}\right)\otimes \cdots \otimes \left(\sum_{i_p} a_{p,i_p}\mathbf{b}^{\,p}_{i_p}\right) \\
 &= \sum_{i_1,\dots,i_p} \left(\prod_{k=1}^p a_{k,i_k}\right) \mathbf{b}^{\,1}_{i_1} \otimes \cdots \otimes \mathbf{b}^{\,p}_{i_p},
\end{aligned}\] 其中系数矩阵秩为 $1.$
（向量的张量积称为简单张量或纯张量，张量的秩定义为能够生成其的最少简单张量的数目。秩-$1$张量即简单张量，秩-$2$张量对应于 $N \times N$ 矩阵。）
