## Chapter 3 Determinants

### 2. What properties determinant should have.

> p.77 Prove that Additivity $$\begin{aligned}
 & D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underbrace{\mathbf{u}_k+\mathbf{v}_k}_k, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n)\\
 = & D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underset{k}{\mathbf{u}_k}, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n) + D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underset{k}{\mathbf{v}_k}, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n)
\end{aligned}$$ can be deduced from Homogeneity $$
 D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underset{k}{\alpha \mathbf{v}_k}, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n) = \alpha D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underset{k}{\mathbf{v}_k}, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n)
 $$ and Shear Invariance $$\begin{aligned}
 & D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underbrace{\mathbf{v}_j + \alpha \mathbf{v}_k}_j, \ldots ,\underset{k}{\mathbf{v}_k}, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n) \\
 = & D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underset{j}{\mathbf{v}_j}, \ldots ,\underset{k}{\mathbf{v}_k}, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n).
\end{aligned}$$

首先易证 $D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_k, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_k, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n) = 0$. 对于 $D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underbrace{\mathbf{u}_k+\mathbf{v}_k}_k, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n)$，若除第 $k$ 位外其他向量线性相关，则原式左右两侧均等于 $0$，得证。若线性无关，则其张成一个 $n-1$ 维超平面；$\mathbf{v}_i \in V^n$ 可以表示为 $\beta_i\mathbf{n} + \mathbf{w}_i$，其中 $\mathbf{n}$ 垂直于超平面，$\mathbf{w}_i$ 为超平面上向量。$$\begin{aligned}
 & D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underbrace{\mathbf{u}_k+\mathbf{v}_k}_k, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n) \\
 = & D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underbrace{(\beta_1 + \beta_2)\mathbf{n} + \mathbf{w}_1 + \mathbf{w}_2}_k, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n) \\
 = & D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underbrace{(\beta_1 + \beta_2)\mathbf{n}}_k, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n) \\
 = & D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underset{k}{\beta_1 \mathbf{n}}, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n) + D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underset{k}{\beta_2 \mathbf{n}}, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n) \\
 = & D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underbrace{\beta_1 \mathbf{n} + \mathbf{w}_1}_k, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n) + D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underbrace{\beta_2 \mathbf{n} + \mathbf{w}_2}_k, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n) \\
 = & D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underset{k}{\mathbf{u}_k}, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n) + D(\mathbf{v}_1, \ldots ,\underset{k}{\mathbf{v}_k}, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n)
\end{aligned}$$

### 3. Constructing the determinant.

> 3.4. A square ($n × n$) matrix is called skew-symmetric (or antisymmetric) if $A^T = −A$. Prove that if $A$ is skew-symmetric and $n$ is odd, then $\det A = 0$. Is this true for even $n$?

$\det A = \det A^T = \det -A = (-1)^n \det A$，故奇数维斜对称矩阵行列式等于 $0$.
对 $n$ 阶斜对称矩阵 $A$，递归定义普夫法(Pfaffian)量 $$ \text{pf}(A) = \begin{dcases} 0, & n=1; \\ [A]_{1,2}, & n=2; \\ \sum^n_{j=2}(-1)^j[A]_{1,j}\text{pf}(A(1,j\mid 1,j)), & n \geq 3 \end{dcases} $$ 其中 $[A]_{i,j}$ 表示 $A$ 的相应元素，$ A(i,j\mid i,j) $ 表示 $A$ 删去 $i$ 行 $j$ 列和 $i$ 列 $j$ 行后的矩阵。
可以证明有 $\det A = \text{pf}(A)^2$.

### 4. Formal deﬁnition. Existence and uniqueness of the determinant.

> 4.2. Let $P$ be a *permutation* matrix, i.e. an $n \times n$ matrix consisting of zeroes and ones and such that there is exactly one 1 in every row and every column.
>> a) Can you describe the corresponding linear transformation? That will explain the name.

若记 $P$ 中第 $i$ 行唯一为 $1$ 的元素在 $p_{i}$ 列，则 $$ P(1, \ldots n)^T = (p_1, \ldots ,p_n)^T, $$ 由 $p_i$ 的唯一性知，$P$ 给出了原序列的一个排列。 

>> b) Show that $P$ is invertible. Can you describe $P^{-1}$ ?

$P$ 可以通过初等操作变为 $I$；原序列到某一排列是一个双射。

>> c) Show that for some $N>0$ $$ P^N:=\underbrace{P P \ldots P}_{N \text { times }}=I. $$ Use the fact that there are only finitely many permutations.

对于 $n=1$，$P = (1)$ 可以遍历所有置换，对于 $n=2$，$P = \begin{pmatrix} 
    0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 
\end{pmatrix}$ 可以遍历所有置换。对于 $n\geq 3$，单个矩阵能生成的最大置换数目即为对称群 $S_n$ 置换最大阶 $g(n)$，兰道（Laudau）证明 $g(n)\rightarrow \exp(\sqrt{n\ln n})$，而置换的总数为 $n!$，于是随 $n$ 增加所需的最少初始“生成矩阵”的数目会趋于阶乘级别。如果允许交叉（$P_1\circ P_2$），两个 $P$ 即可遍历所有置换，例如对换（$2,1$）与轮换（$2,3, \ldots ,n,1$），具体流程想象冒泡排序的逆过程。

### 7. Review exercises for Chapter 3.

> 7.5. (Orientation) Let $\mathbf{v}_1, \mathbf{v}_2$ be vectors in $\mathbb{R}^2$. Show that $D(\mathbf{v}_1, \mathbf{v}_2) > 0$ if and only if there exists a rotation $T_\alpha$ such that the vector $T_\alpha \mathbf{v}_1$ is parallel to $\mathbf{e}_1$ (and looking in the same direction), and $T_\alpha \mathbf{v}_2$ is in the upper half-plane $x_2 > 0$ (the same half-plane as $\mathbf{e}_2$).

$\det T_\alpha = \begin{vmatrix} 
    \cos\alpha & -\sin\alpha \\
    \sin\alpha & \cos\alpha
\end{vmatrix} = 1$（$n$ 维空间中旋转矩阵可一般地定义为行列式为 $1$ 的正交矩阵，若为 $-1$ 则包含了反射，参考 Ch 05 习题 6.8-6.9）
$D(T_\alpha \mathbf{v}_1, T_\alpha \mathbf{v}_2) = \begin{vmatrix}
    v_{1,1}^\prime & v_{2,1}^\prime \\
    0 & v_{2,2}^\prime
\end{vmatrix} = v_{1,1}^\prime v_{2,2}^\prime$，又 $D(T_\alpha \mathbf{v}_1, T_\alpha \mathbf{v}_2) = \det T_\alpha D(\mathbf{v}_1, \mathbf{v}_2) > 0$，$T_\alpha \mathbf{v}_1$ 为 $x$ 正向，$v_{1,1}^\prime > 0$，故 $v_{2,2}^\prime > 0$.
