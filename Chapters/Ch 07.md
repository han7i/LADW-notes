## Chapter 7 Bilinear and quadratic forms

### 1. Main definition

> Lemma 1.1. Let $(A\mathbf{x},\mathbf{x})$ be real for all $\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{C}^n.$ Then $A = A^{\ast}.$ 

由极化恒等式，$\forall \mathbf{x},\mathbf{y} \in \mathbb{C},$ \[\begin{aligned}
 (A\mathbf{x},\mathbf{y}) &= \frac{1}{4}\sum_{\alpha\in \mathbb{C},\alpha^4=1} \alpha(A(\mathbf{x}+\alpha\mathbf{y}),\mathbf{x}+\alpha\mathbf{y}) \\
 &= \frac{1}{4}\sum_{\alpha\in \mathbb{C},\alpha^4=1} \alpha(\mathbf{x}+\alpha\mathbf{y},A(\mathbf{x}+\alpha\mathbf{y})) \\
 &= (\mathbf{x},A\mathbf{y}) = (A^{\ast}\mathbf{x},\mathbf{y}),
\end{aligned}\] 令 $\mathbf{y} = (A - A^{\ast})\mathbf{x}$ 得 $\left\lVert (A - A^{\ast})\mathbf{x} \right\rVert  = 0,$ 即 $A\mathbf{x} = A^{\ast}\mathbf{x},$ 由 $\mathbf{x}$ 的任意性即得。

### 4. Positive definite forms. Minimax characterization of eigenvalues and the Sylvester’s criterion of positivity

> 4.3 Let $A$ be a $2 \times 2$ Hermitian matrix, such that $a_{1,1} > 0, \det A \geq 0.$ Prove that $A$ is positive semidefinite.

$\det A = a_{1,1}a_{2,2} - a_{1,2}\overline{a_{1,2}} \geq 0,$ 故 $\sqrt{a_{1,1}a_{2,2}} \geq |a_{1,2}|;$ 由 $a_{1,1} > 0,$ 知 $a_{2,2} \geq 0,$ 由算术-几何平均不等式， \[\begin{aligned}
 (A\mathbf{x},\mathbf{x}) &= a_{1,1}x_1^2 + a_{1,2}x_1x_2 + \overline{a_{1,2}}x_1x_2 + a_{2,2}x_2^2 \\
 &\geq 2\sqrt{a_{1,1}a_{2,2}x_1^2 x_2^2} - 2|a_{1,2}||x_1x_2|\\
 &= 2|x_1x_2|(\sqrt{a_{1,1}a_{2,2}} - |a_{1,2}|) \geq 0.
\end{aligned}\]
