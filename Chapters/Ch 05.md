### 2. Orthogonality. Orthogonal and orthonormal bases.

> 2.3. Let $\mathbf{v}_1, \mathbf{v}_2, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n$ be an orthonormal basis in $V$.
>> a) Prove that for any $\mathbf{x}=\sum^n_{k=1}\mathbf\alpha_k\mathbf{v}_k,\mathbf{y} = \sum^n_{k=1}\beta_k\mathbf{v}_k,$ \[ (\mathbf{x},\mathbf{y}) = \sum^n_{k=1}\alpha_k\overline{\beta_k} \] 

\[ (\mathbf{x},\mathbf{y}) = \mathbf{y}^*\mathbf{x} = \sum^n_{i=1}\sum^n_{j=1}\alpha_i\overline{\beta_j}(\mathbf{v}_i,\mathbf{v}_j) = \sum^n_{k=1}\alpha_k\overline{\beta_k}(\mathbf{v}_k,\mathbf{v}_k) = \sum^n_{k=1}\alpha_k\overline{\beta_k} \]

> b) Deduce from this the *Parseval's identity* \[ (\mathbf{x},\mathbf{y}) = \sum^n_{k=1}(\mathbf{x},\mathbf{v}_k)\overline{(\mathbf{y},\mathbf{v}_k)} \]

\[ \sum^n_{k=1}(\mathbf{x},\mathbf{v}_k)\overline{(\mathbf{y},\mathbf{v}_k)} =  \sum^n_{k=1}\alpha_k\left\lVert \mathbf{v}_k \right\rVert^2 \overline{\beta_k}\left\lVert \mathbf{v}_k \right\rVert^2 = \sum^n_{k=1}\alpha_k\overline{\beta_k} = (\mathbf{x},\mathbf{y}) \]

> c) Assume now that $\mathbf{v}_1, \mathbf{v}_2, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n$ is only an orthogonal basis, not an orthonormal one. Can you write down Parseval’s identity in this case?

\[ (\mathbf{x},\mathbf{y}) = \sum^n_{k=1}\alpha_k\overline{\beta_k}(\mathbf{v}_k,\mathbf{v}_k)
 = \sum^n_{k=1}\alpha_k \overline{\beta_k}\left\lVert \mathbf{v}_k \right\rVert^2
 = \sum^n_{k=1}\frac{(\mathbf{x},\mathbf{v}_k)\overline{(\mathbf{y},\mathbf{v}_k)}}{\left\lVert \mathbf{v}_k \right\rVert^2} \] 

> This problem shows that if we have an orthonormal basis, we can use the coordinates in this basis absolutely the same way we use the standard coordinates in $\mathbb{C}^n$ (or $\mathbb{R}^n$).
The problem below shows that we can deﬁne an inner product by declaring a basis to be an orthonormal one.

> 2.4. Let $V$ be a vector space and let $\mathbf{v}_1, \mathbf{v}_2, \ldots ,\mathbf{v}_n$ be a basis in $V$. For $\mathbf{x} = \sum^n_{k=1}\alpha_k \mathbf{v}_k,\mathbf{y} = \sum^n_{k=1}\beta_k \mathbf{v}_k$, define $\langle\mathbf{x},\mathbf{y}\rangle:=\sum^n_{k=1}\alpha_k \overline{\beta_k}$.
Prove that $\langle\mathbf{x},\mathbf{y}\rangle$ defines an inner product in $V$.

- 共轭对称性：\[ \left< \mathbf{x},\mathbf{y} \right> = \sum^n_{k=1}\alpha_k \overline{\beta_k} = \overline{\sum^n_{k=1}\overline{\alpha_k} \beta_k} = \overline{\left< \mathbf{y},\mathbf{x} \right>}; \]
- 首位线性：记 $\mathbf{w} = a \mathbf{x} + b \mathbf{y} = \sum^n_{k=1}(a\alpha_k+b\beta_k)\mathbf{v}_k = \sum^n_{k=1}\omega_k \mathbf{v}_k$, \[ \begin{aligned}
 \left< a \mathbf{x} + b \mathbf{y},\mathbf{z} \right> &= \sum^n_{k=1}\omega_k \overline{\gamma_k} = a\sum^n_{k=1}\alpha_k\overline{\gamma_k} + b\sum^n_{k=1}\beta_k\overline{\gamma_k} \\
 &= a\left< \mathbf{x},\mathbf{z} \right> + b\left< \mathbf{y},\mathbf{z} \right>; \end{aligned} \] 
- 正定性：\[ \left< \mathbf{x},\mathbf{x} \right> = \sum^n_{k=1}\alpha_k \overline{\alpha_k} = \sum^n_{k=1}|\alpha_k|^2 \geq 0 ,\] 上式仅在 $\forall k,\alpha_k = 0$ 时取 $0$，此时 $\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}.$
注意到 $\left< \mathbf{v}_i,\mathbf{v}_j \right> = \delta_{ij}$，即 $\{\mathbf{v}_i\}$ 为 $(V,\left< \cdot,\cdot \right>)$ 下的一组规范正交基。

### 3. Orthogonal projection and Gram-Schmidt orthogonalization

> 3.8. Let $P$ be the orthogonal projection onto a subspace $E$ of an inner product space $V$, $\dim V = n, \dim E = r$. Find the eigenvalues and the eigenvectors (eigenspaces). Find the algebraic and geometric multiplicities of each eigenvalue.

令 $P\mathbf{v} = \lambda \mathbf{v}$，$\mathbf{w} = \lambda \mathbf{v} $，有 $\lambda_1 = 1, E(\lambda_1,P) = E$，$\lambda_2 = 0, E(\lambda_2,P) = E^{\perp}$. 注意到 $\dim E + \dim E^{\perp} = \dim V$，故 $P$ 可对角化，代数重数等于几何重数，$\lambda_1$ 重数为 $r$，$\lambda_2$ 重数为 $n-r$.

> 3.9 (Using eigenvalues to compute determinants).
>> a) Find the matrix of the orthogonal projection onto the one-dimensional subspace in $\mathbb{R}^n$ spanned by the vector $(1, 1, \ldots , 1)^T$ ;
>> b) Let $A$ be the $n \times n$ matrix with all entries equal $1$. Compute its eigenvalues and their multiplicities (use the previous problem);

\[ P = \sum^r_{k=1}\frac{1}{\left\lVert \mathbf{v}_k \right\rVert^2}\mathbf{v}_k \mathbf{v}_k^* = \frac{1}{n}J_{n\times n}.\] 由 `a` 及 `3.8`，$\lambda_1 = n$，重数为 $1$，$\lambda_2 = 0$，重数为 $n-1$.

>> c) Compute eigenvalues (and multiplicities) of the matrix $A − I$, i.e. of the matrix with zeroes on the main diagonal and ones everywhere else;
>> d) Compute $\det(A − I)$.

$\lambda_1 = n-1,\lambda_2 = -1,\det(A-I) = (n-1)(-1)^{n-1}$

> 3.10. (Legendre’s polynomials:). Let an inner product on the space of polynomials be defined by $ (f,g) = \int^{1}_{-1} f(t)\overline{g(t)}\,dt  $. Apply Gram-Schmidt orthogonalization to the system $1, t, t^2, t^3$.
Legendre’s polynomials are particular case of the so-called orthogonal polynomials, which play an important role in many branches of mathematics.

\[ \begin{aligned}
 \mathbf{u}_1 &= 1 \\
 \mathbf{u}_2 &= t - \frac{(t,1)}{(1,1)}\cdot 1 = t \\
 \mathbf{u}_3 &= t^2 - \frac{(t^2,1)}{(1,1)}\cdot 1 - \frac{(t^2,t)}{(t,t)}\cdot t = t^2-\frac{1}{3} \\
 \mathbf{u}_4 &= t^3 - \frac{(t^3,1)}{(1,1)}\cdot 1 - \frac{(t^3,t)}{(t,t)}\cdot t - \frac{(t^3,t^2-\frac{1}{3})}{(t^2-\frac{1}{3},t^2-\frac{1}{3})}\cdot (t^2-\frac{1}{3}) \\
  &= t^3-\frac{3}{5}t
\end{aligned} \]（在球坐标中求解三维拉普拉斯方程时问题归结为勒让德方程的求解）

> 3.11 Let $P = P_E$ be the matrix of an orthogonal projection onto a subspace $E$. Show that
>> a) The matrix $P$ is *self-adjoint*, meaning that $P^* = P$.

\[ \begin{aligned}
 (P \mathbf{u},\mathbf{w}) &= \left( \sum^r_{k=1}\frac{(\mathbf{u},\mathbf{v}_k)}{\left\lVert \mathbf{v}_k \right\rVert^2}\mathbf{v}_k,\mathbf{w} \right) = \sum^r_{k=1}\frac{(\mathbf{u},\mathbf{v}_k)}{\left\lVert \mathbf{v}_k \right\rVert^2}(\mathbf{v}_k,\mathbf{w}) \\
 &= \sum^r_{k=1}\frac{(\mathbf{v}_k,\mathbf{w})}{\left\lVert \mathbf{v}_k \right\rVert^2}(\mathbf{u},\mathbf{v}_k) = \overline{(P \mathbf{w},\mathbf{u})} = (\mathbf{u},P \mathbf{w})
 \end{aligned}
\]

>> b) $P^2 = P.$

由定义知 $\operatorname{Im} P_E = E$，又 $P_E \upharpoonright_E = \text{id}_E$

> Remark: The above 2 properties completely characterize orthogonal projection, i.e. any matrix $P$ satisfying these properties is the matrix of some orthogonal projection. We will discuss this some time later.

> 3.12. Show that for a subspace $E$ we have $(E^\perp)^\perp = E$. 

$\forall \mathbf{w}\in E^\perp \, \forall \mathbf{v}\in E,\mathbf{v}\perp \mathbf{w}$，故 $E \subseteq (E^\perp)^\perp$，又 $V = E \oplus E^\perp = E^\perp \oplus (E^\perp)^\perp$，故 $\dim E = \dim (E^\perp)^\perp$，于是 $E = (E^\perp)^\perp$.
（在无穷维向量空间中，由于直和分解只对闭子空间成立，此时 $(E^\perp)^\perp$ 等于闭包 $\overline{E}$）

> Suppose $P$ is the orthogonal projection onto a subspace $E$, and $Q$ is the orthogonal projection onto the orthogonal complement $E^\perp$.
>> a) What are $P + Q$ and $PQ$?

$(P+Q)\mathbf{v} = P \mathbf{v} + Q \mathbf{v} = \mathbf{v}_E + \mathbf{v}_{E^\perp} = \mathbf{v}$，故 $P+Q = I_V$；
$\operatorname{Im}Q = \operatorname{Ker}P = E^\perp$，故 $PQ = \mathbf{0}$.

>> b) Show that $P − Q$ is its own inverse.

$(P-Q)^2 \mathbf{v} = P^2 \mathbf{v} + Q^2 \mathbf{v} - PQ \mathbf{v} - QP \mathbf{v} = (P+Q)\mathbf{v} = \mathbf{v}$

### 4. Least square solution. Formula for the orthogonal projection

> 4.5. (Minimal norm solution) Let an equation $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}$ has a solution, and let $A$ has non-trivial kernel (so the solution is not unique). Prove that
>> a) There exist a unique solution $\mathbf{x}_0$ of $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}$ minimizing the norm $\left\lVert \mathbf{x} \right\rVert $, i.e. that there exists unique $\mathbf{x}_0$ such that $A\mathbf{x}_0 = \mathbf{b}$ and $ \left\lVert \mathbf{x}_0 \right\rVert  \leq \left\lVert \mathbf{x} \right\rVert $ for any $\mathbf{x}$ satisfying $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}$.
>> b) $\mathbf{x}_0 = P_{(\operatorname{Ker}A)^\perp}\mathbf{x}$ for any $\mathbf{x}$ satisfying $A\mathbf{x} = b$.

对任意解 $\mathbf{x}$，可以作如下分解：$\mathbf{x} = P_{\operatorname{Ker}A}\mathbf{x} + P_{(\operatorname{Ker}A)^\perp}\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{x}_1 + \mathbf{x}_2$，由 $A\mathbf{x}_1 = 0$ 知 $\mathbf{x}_2$ 亦为原方程的解，由勾股定理，$\left\lVert \mathbf{x}_2 \right\rVert^2 \leq \left\lVert \mathbf{x} \right\rVert^2 $，仅当 $\mathbf{x}_1 = \mathbf{0}$ 时取等。若存在不等的解 $\mathbf{x}_a, \mathbf{x}_b \in (\operatorname{Ker}A)^\perp$，则非零向量 $\mathbf{x}_a - \mathbf{x}_b \notin \operatorname{Ker}A^\perp$，与子空间 $(\operatorname{Ker}A)^\perp$ 对加法封闭矛盾，故上述构造的 $\mathbf{x}_2$ 是唯一的。
（分析学证法：$A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}$ 的解集是一个闭凸集，而 $f(\mathbf{x}) = \left\lVert \mathbf{x} \right\rVert^2 $ 是一个严格凸函数，严格凸函数在一个凸集上的局部极小值必然是全局极小值，且该极小值点是唯一的）

> 4.6. (Minimal norm least square solution) Applying previous problem to the equation $A\mathbf{x} = P_{\operatorname{Ran}A}\mathbf{b}$ show that
>> a) There exists a unique least square solution $\mathbf{x}_0$ of $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}$ minimizing the norm $\left\lVert \mathbf{x} \right\rVert $.
>> b) $\mathbf{x}_0 = P_{(\operatorname{Ker}A)^\perp}\mathbf{x}$ for any least square solution $\mathbf{x}$ of $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{b}$.

即摩尔-彭罗斯伪逆（Moore-Penrose pseudoinverse）之定义。

### 5. Adjoint of a linear transformation. Fundamental subspaces revisited.

> 5.3. Let $A$ be an $m \times n$ matrix. Show that $\operatorname{Ker} A = \operatorname{Ker}(A^*A)$.
> 5.4. Use the equality $\operatorname{Ker} A = \operatorname{Ker}(A^∗A)$ to prove that
>> b) If $A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}$ has only the trivial solution, $A$ is left invertible. (You can just write a formula for a left inverse).

$\forall \mathbf{x}\in \operatorname{Ker}A, A^*A\mathbf{x} = A^*\mathbf{0} = \mathbf{0}$，故 $\operatorname{Ker}A \subseteq \operatorname{Ker}(A^*A)$；
$\forall \mathbf{x}\in \operatorname{Ker}(A^*A), \left\lVert A\mathbf{x} \right\rVert^2 = (A\mathbf{x},A\mathbf{x}) = (A^*A\mathbf{x},\mathbf{x}) = 0 $，即 $A\mathbf{x} = 0$，故 $\operatorname{Ker}(A^*A) \subseteq \operatorname{Ker}A$.
$\operatorname{Ker}(A^*A) = \operatorname{Ker}A = \{\mathbf{0}\}$，因而方阵 $A^*A$ 可逆，有 $(A^*A)^{-1}A^*A = I$，即 $A$ 左可逆，左逆 $A^{-1}_L = (A^*A)^{-1}A^*$.

> 5.5. Suppose, that for a matrix $A$ the matrix $A^∗A$ is invertible, so the orthogonal projection onto $\operatorname{Ran}A$ is given by the formula $A(A^∗A)^{−1}A^∗$. Can you write formulas for the orthogonal projections onto the other 3 fundamental subspaces ($\operatorname{Ker}A$, $\operatorname{Ker}A^∗$, $\operatorname{Ran}A^∗$)?

$\operatorname{Ker}A = \operatorname{Ker}(A^*A) = \{\mathbf{0}\}, P_{\operatorname{Ker}A} = \mathbf{0}$,
$\operatorname{Ker}A^* = (\operatorname{Ran}A)^\perp, P_{\operatorname{Ker}A^*} = I-A(A^*A)^{-1}A^*$,
$\operatorname{Ran}A^* = (\operatorname{Ker}A)^\perp, P_{\operatorname{Ran}A^*} = I$.

> 5.6. Let a matrix $P$ be self-adjoint ($P^* = P$) and let $P^2 = P$. Show that $P$ is the matrix of an orthogonal projection. 

由 $P^2 = P$ 知 $P\upharpoonright_{\operatorname{Ran}P} = I$，又 $(\operatorname{Ran}P)^\perp = \operatorname{Ker}P^* = \operatorname{Ker}P$，因而 $P$ 是到 $\operatorname{Ran}P$ 的正交投影。
（投影算子被定义为满足 $P^2 = P$ （幂等（idempotent））的算子 $P$，非正交的投影称为斜投影。考察其零化多项式知投影算子可在某组基下表示为 $\begin{pmatrix} 
    I_k & 0 \\ 0 & 0 
\end{pmatrix} .$）
> 对于投影算子 $P, Q$，$P + Q$ 仍为投影算子的充要条件是 $PQ = QP = 0$（$\operatorname{Ran}P\perp \operatorname{Ran}Q$），$P - Q$ 仍为投影算子的充要条件是 $\operatorname{Ran}Q \subseteq \operatorname{Ran}P$，$PQ$ 仍为投影算子的充要条件是 $P, Q$ 可交换。

$(P+Q)^2 = P^2 + Q^2 + PQ + QP = P + Q + PQ + QP$，即 $PQ + QP = 0$，左乘 $P$ 得 $PQ + PQP = 0$，右乘 $P$ 得 $PQP + QP = 0$，即 $PQ = QP = 0$. 或更一般地，考虑特征值。
> （冯・诺依曼交替投影定理）对于投影算子 $P, Q$，$\lim_{n \to \infty}(PQ)^n = P_{\operatorname{Ran}P\cap \operatorname{Ran}Q}$.（即凸集投影（POCS）在线性空间上的情况。）

### 6. Isometries and unitary operators. Unitary and orthogonal matrices.

> 6.6. Let $A$ and $B$ be unitarily equivalent $n\times n$ matrices.
>> a) Prove that $\operatorname{tr}(A^∗A) = \operatorname{tr}(B^∗B)$.
>> b) Use a) to prove that \[ \sum^n_{j,k = 1} \left\lvert A_{j,k} \right\rvert^2 = \sum^n_{j,k = 1} \left\lvert B_{j,k} \right\rvert^2  \] 

\[ 
 \operatorname{tr}(A^*A) = \operatorname{tr}((UBU^*)^*UBU^*) = \operatorname{tr}(UB^*BU^*) = \operatorname{tr}(B^*B), \\
 \operatorname{tr}(A^*A) = \sum^n_{j,k=1}A_{j,k}^*A_{j,k} = \sum^n_{j,k=1}|A_{j,k}|^2
\]

> （弗罗贝尼乌斯范数（Frobenius norm））\[ \left\lVert A \right\rVert_F := \sqrt{\operatorname{tr}(A^*A)} = \sqrt{\sum^m_{j=1}\sum^n_{k=1}|A_{j,k}|^2} \] 

> 6.8. Let $U$ be a $2 \times 2$ orthogonal matrix with $\det U = 1$. Prove that $U$ is a rotation matrix.

记 $U = \begin{pmatrix} 
    a & b \\ c & d 
\end{pmatrix} $，令 $U^*U = I$，即 \[ a^2 + c^2 = b^2 + d^2 = 1, ab + cd = 0.\] 注意正交矩阵定义在 $\mathbb{R}^n$ 上，记 \[ a = \sin\alpha, c = \cos\alpha, \\b = \sin\beta, d = \cos\beta. \] 由 \[ \begin{aligned}
ab + cd = \sin\alpha\sin\beta + \cos\alpha\cos\beta
= \cos(\alpha-\beta) = 0
\end{aligned} \] 知 $\alpha = \beta+(k+\frac{1}{2})\pi$，又 \[ \begin{aligned}
\det U &= ad - bc = \sin\alpha\cos\beta - \cos\alpha\sin\beta = \sin(\alpha-\beta) = 1 
\end{aligned}, \] 故 $k$ 只能取偶数，即 $U = \begin{pmatrix} 
    \sin\alpha & -\cos\alpha \\ \cos\alpha & \sin\alpha
\end{pmatrix} $，故 $U$ 为旋转矩阵。

> 6.9. Let $U$ be a $3 \times 3$ orthogonal matrix with $\det U = 1$. Prove that
>> a) $1$ is an eigenvalue of $U$.
>> b) If $\mathbf{v}_1, \mathbf{v}_2, \mathbf{v}_3$ is an orthonormal basis, such that $U\mathbf{v}_1 = \mathbf{v}_1$, then in this basis the matrix of $U$ is \[ \begin{pmatrix} 
    1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \cos\alpha & -\sin\alpha \\ 0 & \sin\alpha & \cos\alpha 
\end{pmatrix}, \] where $\alpha$ is some angle.

在 $\mathbb{C}$ 下考察 $U$ 的特征多项式，在 $\det U = 1$ 限制下只能为如下情况：$\lambda_1 = 1,\lambda_2 = e^{i\alpha}, \lambda_3 = e^{-i\alpha}$，故 $1$ 总为 $U$ 的特征值（对奇数维均成立）。
考虑实基底与复基底之间的变换： \[ \begin{pmatrix} 
    u \\ w 
\end{pmatrix} = \frac{1}{2}\begin{pmatrix} 
    1 & 1 \\ i & -i 
\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix} 
    u+iw \\ u-iw
\end{pmatrix}, \] 记其中系数矩阵为 $\Gamma$，有 \[ \Gamma \operatorname{diag}(e^{i\alpha},e^{-i\alpha}) \Gamma^{-1} = \begin{pmatrix} 
    \cos\alpha & -\sin\alpha \\ \sin\alpha & \cos\alpha 
\end{pmatrix}. \]

### 7. Rigid motions in $\mathbb{R}^n$

> （马祖尔-乌拉姆定理（Mazur–Ulam theorem））赋范线性空间 $X,Y$ 上保距的满射 $\phi:X\rightarrow Y$ 一定为仿射变换。

> 7.1. Give an example of a rigid motion $T$ in $\mathbb{C}^n$, $T(\mathbf{0}) = \mathbf{0}$, which is not a linear transformation.

由上述马祖尔-乌拉姆定理易知 $T$ 对加法一定保持线性：$T(\mathbf{v}_1 + \mathbf{v}_2) = T(\mathbf{v}_1) + T(\mathbf{v}_2)$. 参考上文 Ch 05 习题 6.8-6.9，$2\times 2$ 正交矩阵只有 $\det = \pm 1$ 两种情况，分别对应旋转（线性）或反射（反线性），因而在 $\mathbb{C}^1$ 中只有这两种情况，后者的一个例子是 $T(\mathbf{v}) = \overline{\mathbf{v}}$，有反标量乘法：$T(\alpha\mathbf{v}) = \overline{\alpha}T(\mathbf{v})$. 在高维 $\mathbb{C}^n$ 中，可以令一部分为线性，一部分为反线性，例如 $T(\mathbf{v}_1, \mathbf{v}_2) = (\mathbf{v}_1, \overline{\mathbf{v}_2})$，最终 $T$ 既非线性也非反线性。

### 8. Complexification and decomplexification

> 8.5. Let $U$ be an orthogonal transformation in a real inner product space $X$, satisfying $U^2 = −I$. Show that in this case $\dim X = 2n$, and that there exists a subspace $E \subseteq X$, $\dim E = n$, and an orthogonal transformation $U_0 : E \rightarrow E^\perp$ such that $U$ in the decomposition $X = E \oplus E^\perp$ is given by the block matrix \[ U = \begin{pmatrix} 
    \mathbf{0} & -U^*_0 \\ U_0 & \mathbf{0}      
\end{pmatrix}. \]

$\det U^2 = (\det U)^2 = 1$，又 $\det U^2 = \det (-I) = (-1)^{\dim X}$，故 $\dim X = 2n$.
$(U\mathbf{v},\mathbf{v}) = (U^2\mathbf{v},U\mathbf{v}) = -(\mathbf{v},U\mathbf{v}) = -(U\mathbf{v},\mathbf{v})$，故 $(U\mathbf{v},\mathbf{v}) = 0, U\mathbf{v}\perp \mathbf{v}$. 任取单位向量 $\mathbf{e}_1$，有 $U\mathbf{e}_1 \perp \mathbf{e}_1$，又 $U^2\mathbf{e}_1 = -\mathbf{e}_1$，故 $W_1 = \operatorname{span}\{\mathbf{e}_1, U\mathbf{e}_1\}$ 是 $X$ 在 $U$ 下的一个不变子空间，而 $W_1^\perp$ 同样是 $U$ 下的不变子空间，取 $\mathbf{e}_2 \in W_1^\perp$ 重复上述操作，最终得到 $X = W_1 \oplus W_2 \oplus \cdots \oplus W_n$，令 $E = \operatorname{span}\{\mathbf{e}_1, \mathbf{e}_2, \ldots ,\mathbf{e}_n\}$，易知 $E^\perp = \operatorname{span}\{U\mathbf{e}_1, U\mathbf{e}_2, \ldots ,U\mathbf{e}_n\}$.
给定直和分解 $E\oplus E^\perp$，易知 $U$ 分块矩阵形如 $\begin{pmatrix} 
    \mathbf{0} & V \\ U_0 & \mathbf{0} 
\end{pmatrix},$ 由 $U^2 = -I$ 知 $U^2\upharpoonright_{E^\perp} = U_0V = -I_{n}$，故 $V = -U_0^{-1} = -U_0^*$.
（在无限维情况下 $\det$ 可能无定义，且 $W_1^\perp$ 不一定为 $U$ 的不变子空间，但仍可证明存在 $E$ 使得 $\dim E$ 与 $\dim E^\perp$ 等势，且 $U$ 可表示为上述分块矩阵。考虑所有相互正交的 $U$-不变二维子空间组成的集合，由包含关系进行排序，由佐恩引理，存在一个最大元，用反证法可知其元素直和为 $X$.）
